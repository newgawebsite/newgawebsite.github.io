We Believe...

The Bible is the very Word of God, free from error, and is the final authority for what we believe and the way we live.

There is only one God. He has revealed Himself in three Persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ is God in the flesh, born of a virgin and living a life with no sin.

Man created good, but willfully (wrong doing) separating himself from God.

Only Jesus can remove the separation between man and God, and then only when an individual personally asks Him to do so.

Water baptism is by immersion and is a testimony to what Christ has done is us.

Holy Communion is a memorial to Christ’s death and His return.

The Baptism in the Holy Spirit with the initial, physical evidence of speaking in languages that have not been learned is for everyone who has trusted in Christ.

God changes the lifestyles of those who trust Him.

The Church is the body of Christ, composed of all who have trusted Christ.

In a divinely called ministry to provide leadership to the Church.

God supernaturally heals.

Christ shall return to earth to rescue His Church from God’s judgment of the earth.

There will be a final judgment of those who died without trusting God.

God will replace the present universe with a new and better universe.
