Free food distributed every 3rd Friday of the month from 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM.

Food is available to everyone regardless of income or residency.

If you are a resident of Kansas and over 60 years of age, you may be eligible to receive a monthly Senior Food Box. Applications must be made to the Kansas Foodbank, who determines eligible income levels. Applications are available at City on a Hill Market. You can also call the church office at 620-783-2349 for more information.
